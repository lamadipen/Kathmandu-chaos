# Kathmandu Chaos v0.1.0 Release Notes

## First Playable

Kathmandu Chaos is a browser arcade driving prototype about Maya, a Kathmandu tempo driver trying to save her family permit by completing five chaotic routes across the valley.

## Included

- Five route levels with traffic, passengers, hazards, landmarks, route intro labels, and finish gates.
- Garage progression with route unlocks, saved best fares, route mastery stars, tempo upgrades, and tempo skins.
- Passenger pickup loop with bright pickup markers, target guide, combo scoring, passenger requests, and personality barks.
- Kathmandu-themed street dressing: bilingual shop signs, Nepali tempo plates, rooftop tanks, marigold garlands, stalls, prayer flags, chowks, temples, bus parks, bridges, and gate arches.
- Optional festival/event routes and red-light checkpoints that can be toggled from the garage.
- Power-ups: Battery Boost, Blessing Shield, Fare Magnet, and Double Fare.
- Mobile touch controls, pause menu, generated audio, route ambience, minimap, speed meter, and results screen.
- Starter Kenney CC0 character models with built-in low-poly fallbacks.

## Known Limitations

- Steering and authored turns are still prototype-feel and will need another handling pass after release.
- Character art is placeholder/stand-in quality, even with Nepali accessory overlays.
- Audio is generated in code, not final recorded Kathmandu ambience.
- Progress is local-only through browser `localStorage`.
- No online leaderboard, analytics, crash reporting, or settings export yet.

## Recommended Release Target

This build is best suited as a first playable prototype for itch.io, a private test link, or a small feedback group.
